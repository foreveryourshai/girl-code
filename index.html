Why I Love Theater

There are many reasons why I love theater. I started theater in 2023; But started having a love for it in 2020, when the Broadway hit Hamilton was released on Disney+. I feel like theater is just a way for me to show all my performing style. From singing and dancing, to talking to an audience and making new friends. Theater also helps me embrace the social butterfly in me.

Why I Love Dance

I’ve been dancing since I was 3 years old. Dance feels like my life. I dance to just be calm, to be happy, and even just when I’m bored. Dancing has been a way for me to let go. Personality, I’m a turner. I’m always in my room or any slick place turning.
