About Me

Hi! I’m Hailey. I’m a 13 year old actress and dancer. I started dancing when I was 3, and I started theater when I was 11. A fun fact about me is that I’ve been dancing for 10 years as of this year. Also I’ve only done 2 shows, my first show was Matilda and I was a part of the ensemble. In my second show I was a part of Madagascar Jr, as Gloria the Hippo. One of the main characters. The styles of dance I do are tap, jazz, ballet, hip-hop, contemporary, lyrical, and my personal favorite musical theater. This portfolio is all about my experiences in dance and theater. Please enjoy learning about me and my two most favorite hobbies.

Why I Love Theater

There are many reasons why I love theater. I started theater in 2023; But started having a love for it in 2020, when the Broadway hit Hamilton was released on Disney+. I feel like theater is just a way for me to show all my performing style. From singing and dancing, to talking to an audience and making new friends. Theater also helps me embrace the social butterfly in me.

Why I Love Dance

I’ve been dancing since I was 3 years old. Dance feels like my life. I dance to just be calm, to be happy, and even just when I’m bored. Dancing has been a way for me to let go. Personality, I’m a turner. I’m always in my room or any slick place turning.
